The Beginning

The world of Kingdom Hearts is huge and sometimes it is difficult to discover where to start. This page contains basic information that will start you on your quest.

  • Sora

    Sora lives on the Destiny Islands with his best friends Riku and Kairi, and all three of them dream of venturing from Destiny Islands to find out what lies beyond. Sora, Riku, and Kairi are inseparable and they play together often on the islands. Sora and Kairi drew pictures of themselves in the Secret Place when they were younger.
